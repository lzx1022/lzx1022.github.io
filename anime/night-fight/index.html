Night Fight

Time: 2013.10

Description: course assignment of Basic Animation Design Ⅰ, Partner: Xianglu Kong

Tools: Canon 7D, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Premiere

Overview

Night Fight is a 2 minutes stop motion animation. It's the assignment work of Basic Animation Design Ⅰ in Zhejiang University. I completed it with Xianglu Kong. This animation tells a story about a drunk man fighting with a passerby.

—Plot Outline—

The night falls, everything goes black. A drunk man staggers out of a pub and walks into the grim street. Suddenly, a gust of wind blows by and blows off his glasses. He finds a shadow is moving towards him when he tries to pick his glasses. He thought that shadow was going to hurt him, so he decides to take actions first. After several rounds, he still can not put that man down. Suddenly, he finds a pillar and decides to use it to give him a final strike. However, when he tries to kick the man, he finds there is a big glass between them. It turns out that it is a huge misunderstanding.
