Lizard Run

Time: 2013.12

Description: course assignment of Basic Animation Design Ⅱ, Partner: Xianglu Kong, Yang Li

Tools: Anime Studio Pro, Adobe Photoshop

Overview

Lizard Run is a 2.5 minutes animation. It's the assignment work of course —— Basic Animation Design Ⅱ in Zhejiang University. I completed it with my classmates Xianglu Kong and Yang Li. This animation tells a story about a lizard running to save his fish. The anime software we used is Anime Studio Pro.

—Plot Outline—

One day, a lizard is walking on the street with his beloved fish. When he is crossing the street, a car runs into him. He falls down, broking his fish tank. He dosen't want to watch his fish die, so he begins running. He must run through the whole city and reach the river as quickly as possible. Finally, he arrives at the river before his fish dies, however, when he puts his fish back into the river, he finds the river is suffering from heavy pollution. It is too late...
